Review

Aiden Silveira 2/7/2023

Spider-Man Homecoming is a great movie in my opinion because it has a story that evryone loves. Itshowed everyone that no matter how old you are or how big you are, you are capable of achieving great things. Tom Holland who played Spider-Man/Peter Parker did an amazing job, he performed all his own stunt and overall put on a perfect performace.

My favorite actor in this moview is definitly Tom Holland who played Spider-Man/Peter Parker. He looks as if he wiuld be Spider-Man in real life the way he took on this role, performing all his own stunts, doing flips around the set.

After All, who wouldnt wanna be Spider-Man?

Trivia

  • Did you know Aunt May's car number plate is AMF-1562. AMF is Amazing Fantasy, 15 is the issue number, and 62 is 1962, the year Spider-Man first appeared
  • Did you know that nineteen-year-old Tom Holland was the youngest actor to be cast as Peter Parker
  • Did you know Tom Holland spent a few days attending classes at the Bronx High School of Science in New York City to prepare for his role as Peter Parker.
