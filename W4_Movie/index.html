About Spider-Man Homecoming

A 15 year old boy by the name of peter parker was bitten by a radioactive spider while touring a science companny. Little did he know that spiderbite would change his life forever. Peter ended up gaining super powers such as increased strength, a spider senses and was able to climb on walls. Peter decided to use his powers for good by being a friendly neighborhood Spider-Man, but later down the road he met Tont stark (Iron Man) who provided peter with a new suite to help him with his friendly spiderman deeds. after aquiring this new suite Peter thought it would be a good idea to try and do more than what he was capable and ended up creating a mess. Peter later on cleaned up his mess and was offered a spot on the Avengers.

Company Credits: Marvel Studios

Release Date: July 7, 2017

Genre: Action/Superhero

Running time: 2h 13m
